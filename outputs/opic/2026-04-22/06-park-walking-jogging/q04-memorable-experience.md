# Park / Walking / Jogging - Memorable Experience

## 문제

- 한국어: 공원에서 기억에 남는 경험을 말해 주세요.
- English: Tell me about a memorable experience at the park.

## 암기 포인트 및 흐름

- 언제/어디서 → 운동 중 본 것 → 잠깐의 고민 → 행동 → 마무리 순서
- 공원에서 우연히 지갑을 발견한 장면을 넣는다
- 양심적으로 행동했다는 느낌으로 끝낸다

## 한국어 전문

일요일 아침에 평소처럼 공원에서 조깅을 했습니다.
스트레칭을 조금 하고 나서 트랙을 따라 달리기 시작했습니다.
한 바퀴를 거의 다 돌았을 때, 트랙 위에서 지갑 하나를 보았습니다.
주변을 둘러봤지만, 아무도 보이지 않았습니다.
지갑을 주워서 안을 확인해 보니 현금이 꽤 많이 들어 있었습니다.
순간적으로는 그 돈이면 며칠 전에 백화점에서 봤던 무선 이어폰을 살 수 있겠다는 생각도 들었습니다.
하지만 지갑 안에 신분증도 있어서 주인을 찾을 수 있겠다고 생각했습니다.
심장이 조금 빨리 뛰고 죄책감도 느껴졌지만, 결국 경찰서에 가져다주기로 했습니다.
쉽지 않은 결정이었지만, 그때는 정말 옳은 일을 했다고 생각합니다.

## 영어 전문

One Sunday morning, I went jogging in the park as usual.
After some stretching, I started running on the track.
When I was almost done with one lap, I saw a wallet on the track.
I looked around to see if anyone had dropped it, but nobody was nearby.
I picked it up and looked inside, and to my surprise, there was a lot of cash.
For a moment, I even thought about the wireless earphones I had seen at the department store the other day.
But there was also an ID card in the wallet, so I knew the owner could be found easily.
My heart was pounding, and I felt guilty, but I finally decided to take it to the police station.
It was not an easy decision, but I think I did the right thing at that time.

## 키 문장 및 단어

### Key Sentences

- I went jogging in the park as usual.
- I saw a wallet on the track.
- There was a lot of cash inside.
- My heart was pounding.
- I decided to take it to the police station.

### Key Words

- stretch : 스트레칭하다
- wallet : 지갑
- cash : 현금
- guilty : 죄책감이 드는
- ID card : 신분증
- police station : 경찰서

## 키 한국어 문장 및 대응되는 영어표현 여러개

### 일요일 아침에 공원에서 조깅했습니다.

- I went jogging in the park as usual.
- I was jogging in the park on a Sunday morning.
- I ran at the park like I always do.

### 트랙 위에서 지갑을 발견했습니다.

- I saw a wallet on the track.
- I found a wallet while I was running.
- There was a wallet lying on the track.

### 안을 보니 현금이 많이 들어 있었습니다.

- There was a lot of cash inside.
- I was surprised to see so much money.
- The wallet had a lot of cash in it.

### 순간적으로 다른 생각도 들었습니다.

- For a moment, I thought about keeping it.
- I even thought about buying something with that money.
- I had a brief temptation, but I changed my mind.

### 결국 경찰서에 가져다주기로 했습니다.

- I decided to take it to the police station.
- I turned it in to the police.
- I wanted to do the right thing.

### 그때는 옳은 일을 했다고 생각합니다.

- I think I did the right thing at that time.
- It was not an easy decision, but I felt better after that.
- I was proud that I chose the honest way.

## Follow-up Hooks

- Did you try to find the owner first?
- What did the police say?
- Have you ever found something valuable before?
- Do you often jog in the park on weekends?
