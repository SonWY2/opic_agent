# Home / Apartment - Past vs Present

## 문제

- 한국어: 예전과 비교해서 집 생활이 어떻게 달라졌는지 말해 주세요.
- English: Tell me how your home life has changed over time.

## 암기 포인트 및 흐름

- 어릴 때 단독주택 -> 작은 구조 -> 정원 기억 -> 지금 8층 아파트 -> 장단점
- 예전 집의 정원과 현재 아파트의 편리함을 대비한다
- 현재 집은 편하지만 층간소음과 정원 부재가 아쉽다고 닫는다

## 한국어 전문

어릴 때 저는 단층 주택에서 살았습니다.
그 집에는 방 두 개, 거실, 주방, 욕실이 있었고, 아주 크지는 않았지만 가족이 살기에는 충분했습니다.
제가 특히 좋아했던 점은 작은 정원이 있었다는 것입니다.
어머니가 그곳에서 채소와 꽃을 키우셨고, 저는 그 근처에서 많은 시간을 보냈습니다.
지금은 주거 지역에 있는 높은 아파트 8층에서 가족과 함께 살고 있습니다.
현재 집은 방 네 개, 거실, 주방, 욕실 두 개가 있어서 예전보다 훨씬 넓고 편합니다.
하지만 가끔 층간소음 때문에 스트레스를 받을 때가 있습니다.
또 어릴 때 집처럼 정원이 없다는 점은 조금 아쉽습니다.
그래도 지금 집은 가족 생활과 제 업무 루틴에 잘 맞아서 전반적으로 만족합니다.

## 영어 전문

When I was young, I used to live in a single-story house.
It had two bedrooms, a living room, a kitchen, and a bathroom, and it was not very big, but it was enough for a family.
One thing I especially liked about my old house was that it had a small garden.
My mother grew vegetables and flowers there, and I spent a lot of time around that area.
Now I live with my family on the eighth floor of a tall apartment building in a residential area.
My current home has four rooms, a living room, a kitchen, and two bathrooms, so it is much bigger and more convenient than before.
However, I sometimes get stressed out about inter-floor noise.
Also, I miss having a garden like the one we had when I was a child.
Still, my current home fits my family life and work routine well, so overall I am satisfied with it.

## 키 문장 및 단어

### Key Sentences

- I used to live in a single-story house.
- My old house had a small garden.
- Now I live on the eighth floor of an apartment building.
- I sometimes get stressed out about inter-floor noise.
- I miss having a garden.

### Key Words

- single-story house : 단층 주택
- garden : 정원
- residential area : 주거 지역
- inter-floor noise : 층간소음

## 키 한국어 문장 및 대응되는 영어표현 여러개

### 어릴 때 단층 주택에서 살았습니다.

- I used to live in a single-story house.
- When I was young, I lived in a one-story house.
- My old home was a small single-story house.

### 예전 집에는 작은 정원이 있었습니다.

- My old house had a small garden.
- There was a small garden at my old house.
- One thing I liked about my old home was the garden.

### 지금은 아파트 8층에 살고 있습니다.

- Now I live on the eighth floor of an apartment building.
- My current home is on the eighth floor.
- These days, I live in a tall apartment building.

### 가끔 층간소음 때문에 스트레스를 받습니다.

- I sometimes get stressed out about inter-floor noise.
- Noise between floors can be stressful sometimes.
- Apartment noise can bother me from time to time.

### 어릴 때처럼 정원이 있었으면 좋겠습니다.

- I miss having a garden like the one we had when I was young.
- I sometimes wish our family had a small garden.
- It would be nice to have a garden again.

## Follow-up Hooks

- What was your old home like?
- Did your old home have a garden?
- Is your current apartment bigger?
- Do you have inter-floor noise?
