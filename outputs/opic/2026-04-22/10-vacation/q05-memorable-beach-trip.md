# Vacation - Memorable Beach Trip

## 문제

- 한국어: 기억에 남는 해변 여행 경험을 말해 주세요.
- English: Talk about a memorable beach trip.

## 암기 포인트 및 흐름

- 어릴 때 가족 여행 -> 제주 해변 -> 물놀이/모래성 -> 현지 식당 -> 느낌
- emerald colored ocean, wide white beach, local food 표현을 반복한다.
- 수영 후 배고파서 음식이 더 맛있었다는 쉬운 감정으로 마무리한다.

## 한국어 전문

어릴 때 가족과 제주도 해변에 갔던 여행이 기억에 남습니다.

제주는 한국에서 가장 아름다운 해변이 있는 곳 중 하나라고 생각합니다.

그때 본 바다는 에메랄드빛이었고, 물도 정말 깨끗해 보였습니다.

저는 가족과 함께 하루 종일 수영을 하고, 해변에서 모래성을 만들며 놀았습니다.

넓고 하얀 해변이라서 아이들이 놀기에도 좋고, 그냥 걷기에도 좋았습니다.

물놀이를 한 뒤에는 해변 근처의 현지 식당에 갔습니다.

너무 배가 고파서 그런지, 그날 먹은 음식이 정말 맛있게 느껴졌습니다.

특히 고기가 부드럽고 육즙이 있어서 아직도 기억이 납니다.

그 여행 이후로 저는 해변 여행을 생각하면 제주도와 그 식당이 먼저 떠오릅니다.

## 영어 전문

I remember going to a beach in Jeju with my family when I was a child.

I think Jeju has some of the most beautiful beaches in Korea.

The ocean I saw back then was emerald colored, and the water looked very clean.

I spent the whole day swimming with my family and making sand castles on the beach.

The beach was wide and white, so it was good for children to play and also nice for walking.

After playing in the water, we went to a local restaurant near the beach.

Maybe because I was so hungry, the food tasted really amazing that day.

I especially remember the meat because it was tender and juicy.

Since that trip, when I think of beach vacations, Jeju and that restaurant come to my mind first.

## 키 문장 및 단어

### Key Sentences

- I remember going to a beach in Jeju with my family.
- The ocean was emerald colored.
- I spent the whole day swimming.
- We went to a local restaurant near the beach.
- The food tasted amazing because I was hungry.

### Key Words

- emerald colored : 에메랄드빛의
- sand castle : 모래성
- local restaurant : 현지 식당
- tender : 부드러운
- juicy : 육즙이 있는

## 키 한국어 문장 및 대응되는 영어표현 여러개

### 가족과 제주도 해변에 갔던 일이 기억납니다.

- I remember going to a beach in Jeju with my family.
- I still remember a family beach trip to Jeju.
- A beach trip to Jeju with my family stays in my memory.

### 바다가 에메랄드빛이고 깨끗했습니다.

- The ocean was emerald colored and clean.
- The sea had a beautiful emerald color.
- The water looked clear and beautiful.

### 하루 종일 수영하고 모래성을 만들었습니다.

- I spent the whole day swimming and making sand castles.
- I swam and built sand castles all day.
- We enjoyed the beach by swimming and playing in the sand.

### 현지 식당에서 먹은 음식이 정말 맛있었습니다.

- The food at the local restaurant tasted amazing.
- The local food was really good.
- The meal near the beach was one of the best parts of the trip.

### 너무 배고파서 음식이 더 맛있게 느껴졌습니다.

- The food tasted even better because I was so hungry.
- Since I was starving after swimming, everything tasted great.
- After playing in the water, the meal felt especially delicious.

## Follow-up Hooks

- When did you go to Jeju?
- What did you do at the beach?
- Did you try local food?
- Why was the trip memorable?
