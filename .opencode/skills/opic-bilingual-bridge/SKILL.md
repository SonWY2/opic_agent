## What it does
- Builds Korean full scripts from English topic answers
- Extracts key Korean lines
- Maps each Korean line to multiple English variants
- Preserves story facts while varying expression patterns

## Variant rules
For each key Korean sentence:
- provide 2 to 4 English variants
- keep the same core meaning
- do not change the persona facts
- vary structure, tone, or connector lightly
- keep all variants realistic for spoken OPIc use

## Good variant example
Korean:
- 주말에는 보통 근처 카페에 가서 커피를 마시며 쉬어요.

English variants:
- On weekends, I usually go to a nearby cafe and relax with a cup of coffee.
- I often spend my weekend at a cafe near my home and enjoy some coffee.
- A nearby cafe is one of my favorite places on weekends, because I can sit down and relax with coffee there.
