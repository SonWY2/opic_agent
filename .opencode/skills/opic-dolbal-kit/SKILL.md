## What it does
- Gives a fallback structure for unexpected topics
- Connects unfamiliar themes to familiar personal experience
- Prevents blanking out

## Safe surprise-topic frame
1. simple local fact
2. personal connection
3. one short episode
4. simple opinion or trend

## Good surprise-topic buckets
- recycling
- internet / devices
- weather / seasons
- holidays
- public places like banks restaurants hospitals

## Rule
If knowledge is weak, stay personal and concrete instead of trying to sound expert.
